Wealth Management

Investment Advisory

HPM Partners is an independent investment advisor dedicated to providing objective investment advisory and consulting services and to the establishment of long-term relationships with our clients. Our firm was co-founded with the Milstein family, widely acknowledged for their success over three generations in investing, real estate, banking, manufacturing, energy and international business.

Our clients may choose HPM Partners to consult on an existing investment program or to implement a new one. Our team is composed of experienced, credentialed and dedicated professionals who offer objective and personalized advice. Our clients benefit from our collective investment experience, access to our premier global strategic partners and the highest standards of excellence.

Wealth Planning

We work with our clients’ other advisors to develop cohesive recommendations, and coordinate the implementation of these recommendations to meet short, intermediate and long-term objectives. Our wealth planning services include an annual review of:

  • Taxable estate, net worth, liquidity, and cash flow
  • Property titling, beneficiary designations, incidents of ownership, and powers of appointment
  • Gifting strategies, including the use of annual exclusions, the lifetime unified credit, and appreciated assets
  • Insurance policies, including auto, home, personal property, personal liability, medical, life, disability, and long-term care
  • Compensation and benefit plans
  • Plans for funding education, retirement, and/or long-term health care expenses and
  • Charitable gifting

Tax Planning and Compliance

We work with our clients to develop strategies which seek to reduce current and future tax liabilities and comply with all income, estate and gift tax regulations. We integrate these strategies with our clients’ financial and investment plans. Our tax services include:

  • Planning to minimize current and future income, capital gain, gifting, and estate taxes
  • Planning for charitable gifting
  • Creation of annual and multi-year tax projections
  • Determination of estimated quarterly tax payments
  • Preparation of individual, trust, and entity tax returns.

Lifestyle Management

We seek to simplify our clients’ lives and reduce the time and burden involved with cash management, reviewing and paying bills, obtaining financing for certain purchases, and debt management. Our lifestyle management services include:

  • Preparation and review of monthly, annual, and multi-year cash flow budgets detailing living expenses and wealth accumulation
  • Receipt, review, and payment of monthly bills
  • Maintenance and management of cash balances

Trust Services

We offer a broad range of fiduciary services, including trust and estate administration services, through HPM Partners Trust Services, a division of our affiliate New York Private Trust Company. Unlike many other trust companies, we accept and are able to manage trusts containing non-traditional assets such as real estate, oil and gas interests, and operating businesses. Our trust services include:

  • Administrative or Full Trustee Services for:
    • Charitable Remainder or Foundation Trusts
    • Life Insurance Trusts
    • IRA Accounts
    • Dynastic Trusts
    • Asset Protection Trusts and
    • Non-Traditional Trust Assets
  • Estate Administration Services (Executor or Co-Executor)
  • Escrow Services

Private Capital Finance

We provide our clients with a clear understanding of their financial position and the capital that may be required to maintain their lifestyle, achieve certain liquidity, fund new investment opportunities and/or expand business operations. Our private capital finance services include:

  • Assessment of all assets and liabilities
  • Analysis of the terms, cost and structure of existing loans
  • Identification of best financing solutions and
  • Negotiation of pricing, terms, and covenants
