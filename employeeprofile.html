Irvin A. Leonard

Partner
Managing Partner

Cleveland
(216)687-0700

New York
(212)850-4290

Irv is the Managing Partner and a member of the Investment Committee.

Prior to joining HPM Partners, Irv was a Senior Advisor of Emigrant Bank and a Director of New York Private Trust Company. He continues in those roles and also serves as Chief Executive Officer of New York Private Trust Company.

He joined Emigrant Bank in 2008 upon his retirement as a partner at the law firm of Jones Day, an international law firm with more than 2,400 attorneys. During his thirty-eight-year career with Jones Day, he was a member of the firm’s corporate, real estate and health care practices. Irv has extensive experience in the representation of privately held businesses, as well as private equity and venture capital funds.

For more than a decade, Irv served as Jones Day’s Chief Financial Partner. He also chaired the Firm’s investment committee, leading the development and administration of the investment policies and strategies for management of the Firm’s sizeable defined benefit plan and defined contribution plan assets. Prior to joining Jones Day, Irv was a law clerk to Judge Inzer B. Wyatt, United States District Court for the Southern District of New York.

Irv received his B.A. from the University of Pittsburgh, Phi Beta Kappa, magna cum laude, and his J.D. from New York University School of Law, Order of the Coif, Vanderbilt Medal. He is a member of the American Bar Association, serves on the Board of Trustees and Executive Committee of the Greater Cleveland Chapter of the American Red Cross and is a Past President and Trustee for Life of the Jewish Education Center of Cleveland.
