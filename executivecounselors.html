Executive Financial Counselors

Executives are entrusted by shareholders to grow and protect shareholder value. They focus on growing market share, developing talent, delivering product and service innovation, and managing risks. This dedication results in little time, interest, and/or expertise to effectively manage the complexities of their growing personal wealth. Recognizing this constraint, we developed Executive Financial Counseling, a comprehensive and integrated financial advisory service for executives of corporations and partners of professional services firms.

We work with senior executives of Fortune 1000 companies and partners of major law, management consulting, investment banking, and medical firms to accomplish objectives such as:

Maximizing Company Benefits

  • Review effective use of health and welfare plans
  • Ensure maximum benefit from 401(k), defined benefit, and non-qualified retirement plans
  • Assess equity and option strategies, including 10b5-1 plans

Minimizing Taxes

  • Monitor and review withholdings
  • Prepare estimated tax payments and prepare returns
  • Identify opportunities to minimize current and future tax liabilities, including issues relating to 83b elections, 280g, and exercising stock options

Investing for Lifestyle and Education

  • Project and manage cash flows; evaluate actual expenditures versus plan
  • Develop a customized investment program based on goals and risk-return expectations
  • Select third-party investment managers and oversee investment program

Planning for Retirement

  • Assist in all plan distribution and investment elections
  • Assess use and benefit of qualified investment vehicles, such as an IRA or Roth IRA
  • Evaluate Medicare, Social Security, Health Insurance, and Long-Term Care decisions

Transferring Wealth

  • Review and validate effectiveness of current estate plan
  • Ensure that titling and beneficiary designations are appropriate
  • Examine and explore gifting strategies

Managing Risk

  • Review Property, Casualty and Excess liability policies
  • Review Life, Disability, Health and Long-term Care policies
  • Prepare adequacy and cost effectiveness analysis
